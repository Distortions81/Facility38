Help / Controls:

Movement:[W A S D] keys, or [middle-click-drag].
Hold [SHIFT] for increased speed.

Create objects: [left-click]
Delete objects: [right-click]
Zoom: [+] and [-] keys, or [scroll-wheel].

Rotate object (CW): [R] key
Reverse Rotate (CCW): [Shift] + [R]
Quit: [ALT] + [F4] or options menu

[F1]: Help/Controls [F2]: Options [F3]: Toggle Info-Overlay
[F5]: Toggle Resource Layer
