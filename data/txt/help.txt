
CONTROLS:

Camera: WASD keys or middle-click + drag
Run: Shift

Create objects: left-click
Destroy objects: right-click
Zoom: +/- or scroll

Rotate object or toolbar item: R-Key
Reverse Rotate (counter-clock): Shift-R
Quit: ESC or alt-F4

F1: Options, F2: Toggle Info-Overlay
F3: Toggle Resource Layer
F5: Quicksave, F6: Quickload
